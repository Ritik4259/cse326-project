About Us

  • My Personal Finance Manager is a web application that helps you track your income, expenses, and assets.
    • It is a simple and easy-to-use tool that can help you take control of your finances and reach your financial goals.

  • We believe that everyone should have the tools they need to manage their finances effectively.
    • That's why we created My Personal Finance Manager, a free and accessible tool for everyone.

    Features

    • Track your income and expenses
    • Create and track a budget
    • Set financial goals and track your progress
    • Generate reports to see your financial trends
